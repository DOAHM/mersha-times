Ful.Mi.NATION Declares State of Emergency as Irregulars Overrun Major Cities

A massive Irregular outbreak has swept across Ful.Mi.NATION, forcing the government to declare a nationwide state of emergency. Marshal Sasha Vlada has assumed direct command and ordered a full military mobilization. The Kingdom of the White Falcon, bound by a peace accord, is closely monitoring the crisis.

Irregulars emerged simultaneously in Ful.Mi.NATION's capital and several major cities, sending residents into widespread panic. Marshal Sasha Vlada immediately deployed all available forces, and Ful.Mi.NATION has signaled it intends to resolve the crisis with its own military without requesting external assistance. The Kingdom of the White Falcon convened an emergency security council chaired by Princess Stella within hours of the first reports.

"The stability of Ful.Mi.NATION is directly linked to Mirsha's stability. We are monitoring the situation closely." — 1st Army Command
